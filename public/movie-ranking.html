The Best Movies According to Tijana

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar (2014)

A determined astronaut ventures through space and time to find humanity a new home as Earth faces its final years, testing the bonds between father and daughter.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A wrongfully convicted banker forms a deep friendship while serving a life sentence in prison, never losing hope as he carefully crafts an ingenious plan for his freedom over two decades.

The Grand Budapest Hotel (2014)

A charismatic hotel concierge and his young protégé become entangled in a whimsical adventure.
